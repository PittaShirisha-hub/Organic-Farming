Organic Farming in Mizoram

Mizoram has been a pioneer in organic farming since 1996, promoting chemical-free agriculture through supportive policies, farmer training, and cultivation of high-value crops like ginger, turmeric, and passion fruit.

Mizoram's key organic crops include ginger, turmeric, chili, passion fruit, orange, pineapple, maize, and rice, along with vegetables and medicinal plants.

Highlights

  • Declared “Organic State” with strong political and administrative commitment.
  • Government Support through schemes like PKVY and HAFED for training, certification, and market access.
  • Mizoram promotes natural and traditional farming with minimal chemical use, focusing on eco-friendly cultivation of diverse crops in tribal and hilly regions.

Support Programs

Mizoram organic farming through the Organic Farming Mission, PKVY, PGS certification, local organic markets, and farmer training programs.
