Organic Farming in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is steadily growing its footprint in organic farming. The state promotes eco-friendly agriculture practices and encourages farmers to use natural fertilizers and pest management systems.

Districts like Erode, Dindigul, and Coimbatore are known for organic cultivation of millets, turmeric, traditional rice varieties, and vegetables. The Tamil Nadu Organic Certification Department (TNOCD) plays a vital role in regulating and supporting organic standards.

The state also sees an increase in organic retail stores and consumer awareness about chemical-free food. Various government schemes help small and marginal farmers transition to organic farming systems.
