Organic Farming in Maharashtra

From Cotton to Turmeric – Sustainability in the Heart of India

Key Organic Products

Maharashtra is a pioneer in promoting organic agriculture, especially in regions like Vidarbha, Pune, and Nashik. Key organic crops include cotton, turmeric, pulses, vegetables, and oilseeds. These crops are cultivated using sustainable techniques that avoid harmful chemicals and protect soil health.

Initiatives and Highlights

The Maharashtra Organic Farming Policy encourages eco-friendly agriculture through financial support, farmer training, certification subsidies, and direct market linkages.

Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) and cooperatives empower smallholders to collectively market their produce, improve incomes, and reach premium organic markets.

Maharashtra's shift toward organic farming strengthens environmental health, farmer welfare, and consumer confidence in sustainably grown food.
