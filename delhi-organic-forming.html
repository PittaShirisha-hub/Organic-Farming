Organic Farming in Delhi

As India’s capital, Delhi is embracing urban and peri-urban organic farming practices to improve sustainability, health, and food security. The city promotes rooftop farming, kitchen gardens, and chemical-free produce in collaboration with local bodies and NGOs.

Despite limited agricultural space, Delhi's residents are turning to organic farming through innovative approaches like composting, vertical farming, and hydroponics.

Highlights

  • Promotion of rooftop gardens and terrace farming in urban homes and schools.
  • Organic haats (markets) supported by the Delhi government for local farmers.
  • Workshops and awareness programs on chemical-free cultivation.

Support Programs

Delhi's government and civic bodies collaborate with NGOs to provide seeds, compost units, and training to promote sustainable and eco-friendly urban agriculture.
